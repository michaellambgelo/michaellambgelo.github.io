## Why we're doing this

JXN Film Club runs on two tracks: the podcast and in-person events. Both have real costs and both are picking up.

On the podcast side, the most recent episode is an Animated Features deep dive — the kind of long-form conversation that justifies the recording setup and the editing time. ([jxnfilm.club](https://jxnfilm.club) keeps the episode list current on its home page.) On the events side, we put on a screening of *Hamnet* in February of this year, which was the biggest in-person turnout the club has had to date.

Most of this has been out of pocket on my end since [the podcast revival in 2023]({{ site.url }}/2023/07/jxnfilmclub.html). Stickers are a low-friction way for people who care about local film community to back it. You get something to slap on a laptop or water bottle; the club gets enough runway to keep showing up for the next podcast guest and the next screening.
