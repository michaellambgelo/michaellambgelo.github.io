About

Michael Lamb

Software engineer in Jackson, Mississippi. Writing about distributed systems, side projects, and the rest of life.

Michael is a software engineer at C Spire. He organizes meetups with Jackson Film Club, Jackson Devs, and C Spire Gaming around the Jackson area.

This blog started as a documentation log for a Raspberry Pi cluster build. Most content since then has been about that environment, with community and professional posts mixed in — plus a little personality.

If something here inspires you, I’d love to know about it. Reach out at {{ site.email }}, or start with Hello, World! — the first post.
